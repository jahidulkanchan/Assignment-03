Know more about us

Frequently Asked Question


Find answers to common questions about our services, process, and more. Our FAQ section is designed to provide you with quick and clear information.



What services do you offer?

We provide custom house designs, interior planning, 3D renderings, and renovation consultations tailored to your needs.

How long does the design process take?

The timeline varies depending on the project's complexity, but typically it ranges from 2 to 6 weeks.

Can I make changes during the design process?

Yes, we encourage client input and offer revision opportunities to ensure the final design meets your expectations.

What are your fees for house design?

Our fees are based on the project's scope and complexity. We offer a free initial consultation to discuss your needs and provide a customized quote.
